  • Mar. 12, 2015.

    Dear Authors and Participants of the previous Power Electronics symposiums,
    on the occasion of the 40 years anniversary of the International symposium on Power Electronics and based on decision made by the Assembly of the Society for the Power Electronics (Serbia), we would like to inform you that on the web page of the symposium www.dee.uns.ac.rs you may brows and download all the papers which have been published in the conference proceedings since 1973.
    Paper are in the PDF format and they are available from Monday, March 9th, 2015 under Bibliography of Ee Papers menu.
    Also, I would like to invite you to submit your unpublished papers for the 18th International Symposium on Power Electronic to be held in Novi Sad, Serbia on October 28-30, 2015. Deadline for the submission of extended abstracts is May 24, 2015! Please find more information about the symposium on the web page www.dee.uns.ac.rs
    With Regards, Prof. Vladimir Katic President of the Society for the Power Electronics (Serbia)
